CLEANTECH offers CTWM Services, which is a waste management service that delivers robust and sustainable waste management solutions
for both local government units and large businesses.CTWM Services basically includes waste analyzation and characterization study (WACS),
logistic planning, and hauling & disposal of waste. Service Inclusion and Description:

Waste Analyzation and Characterization Study (WACS)

it is a process wherein the waste generation as well as the composition and physical and chemical properties of the waste are analyzed. By conducting WACS, the amount of waste generated can be reduced, materials that have value can be reused, recovered and recycled, and it can also minimize the waste that can be treated or disposed of.

Logistic Planning

Waste management logistics solutions are designed to maximize the use of infrastructure and technical resources, reduce costs, protect workplace safety and health, and meet legal requirements.

Hauling and Disposal of Waste

Hauling and disposal services include transporting waste across large distances and disposing of it in a partner sanitary landfill or material recovery facility.

The WACS, logistic planning, and hauling and disposal can be availed as a package, while hauling and disposal can be availed alone, both through contract agreements.
